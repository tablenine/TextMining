Can't help it, this is my fave of the bunch!  I like the thin crust, I like the Chicago style.  The crust is crispy and delicious, the sauce is tomato-y, and the cheese is delicious!  What's more to say...when people ask me for my favorite "chicago-style pizza", I direct them here!
