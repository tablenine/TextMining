We love Los Nopales and eat there once or twice a month.  It is a cute, clean, friendly atmosphere.  The owner knows our kids' names.  It is BYOB.  I love the tortilla soup- it's spicy.  The chicken enchiladas mole are delicious.  The shrimp dishes are also our fave.
