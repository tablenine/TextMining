I always get the 1/4 chicken southern style with 2 waffles (it's called scoe's on the menu), yummy. The sides here are really good too. I especially like the mac n' cheese and the candied yams.. . Unfortunately, the cornbread and the red beans aren't the best; I've had much better elsewhere.
