Looking for comfort food in a cool atmosphere?  Silver Cloud is definitely the place to go.  Where else can you find Frito Pie on the menu?  Not to mention the best Sloppy Joe's?  Also, if you ever have a yearning for your old camping days, they even serve S'Mores for dessert!  Well done.
