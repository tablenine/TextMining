One of my favorite places in the city! Probably responsible for getting me hooked on Chang beer and Sriracha chili sauce. The #14 veggie soup loaded with hoisin and oyster sauce is enough to make you forget the billboard dwelling pigeons that are laying waste to your car in the parking lot.
