Stopped here to try the happy hour.  Home made sangria, $4 is just okay, while the happy hour menu was full of cheap fillers, like potatoes and bread.  Sampled the meatball sliders, $5 and the cheese fondu, quite blah.  Nice decor, comfy outside patio is the only real positive from my experience.
