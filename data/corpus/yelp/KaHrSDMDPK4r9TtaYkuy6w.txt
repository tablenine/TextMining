Restaurant: Canton House Chinese Restaurant
Category: Chinese
Decent, albeit greasy dim sum.  It's crowded on weekends, but the wait is never long.  Wait service leaves something to be desired - they tend to yell across the room and can be impatient.  While it's not the best dim sum around, it's a fast, fun place to go when you plan on overeating.
