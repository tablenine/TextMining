The best bar in Atlanta.  Weeknights are usually better than weekends (less suburbanites).  Great prices, great food and great music.  You're almost guaranteed to see something unusual from time to time.  If you're not a smoker (or even if you are) the back room can be a bit harsh but tolerable.
