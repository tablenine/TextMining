The drinks may be expensive, but the view is soooo worth it.  Located at the tippy top of the Shelburne Hotel, this thus far sleepy rooftop bar is bound to catch on soon.  The roomy space and comfy decor make it a sure fire hit whether you're on a date or with the gals.
