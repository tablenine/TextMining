This is an excellent cuban restaurant.  A lot of people seem to be recommending the garlic chicken, but I have to put in my vote for a dish called "ropa vieja".  Basically this is very tender braised beef.  Give it a shot.  Although the food is good it's not exactly the most upscale joint.
