Restaurant: Stanley&#39;s Kitchen and Tap

One of my favorite places for brunch.  It may get crowded and there may be a line, but the atmosphere is full of fun and excitement.  The brunch buffet has a full selection: omelettes, waffles, biscuits and gravy, sausages, bacon, sandwich fixins, cereal, pastries, fruit and more.  Make your own Bloody Mary for a complete meal!  . The servers are often harried, trying to clear plates and refill coffees, but are responsive to your request.
