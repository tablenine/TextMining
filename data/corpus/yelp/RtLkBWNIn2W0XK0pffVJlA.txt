Mmmmmm, soup dumplings.  For the XLB (aka soup dumplings) I give this place 4 stars  - just because those things are so damn hard to find unless your trek deep into the SG Valley. The rest of the dim sum was OK, nothing great, nothing bad, but the soup dumplings were excellent.
