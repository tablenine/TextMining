The food was very delicious and fresh. It wasn't cheap, but it wasn't crazy expensive either. I got the seafood risotto, which was definitely enough to share if you ordered and appetizer, and it was perfect. Not too heavy on the sauce and the seafood was tender. Good wine specials too.
