Restaurant: Two Urban Licks

The food here is amazing!  The best mac n cheese I've ever had.  The pork is the most tender piece of meat I've ever had in my life.  The appetizers are all amazing as well.  The desserts are good too, but the apps and entrees are what this place is all about.  The only reason I didn't give it 5 stars is because it is always too loud.  Not really a good first date place because you can barely hear your company.  I have sat outside before and that isn't nearly as loud.  The parking lot is also ridiculously bumpy.
