The food is a mixed bag here, but the happy hour (4-7 M-F) is amazing, frat boy free and worth 4 stars by itself. $2   22 ounce Rouge Stouts. They also have a great sidewalk patio to people watch.  They are somewhat dog friendly too - must sit at tables near the sidewalk.
