Very cool urban space that serves very good and comforting breakfasts.  The usual brunch menu includes pancakes, omelettes and hash. Vegetarians will love the vegetarian biscuits and gravy with soysage.  Weekend specials can include shrimp and grits and bananas foster french toast.
