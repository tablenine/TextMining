Restaurant: Cobras &amp; Matadors

This is an authentic tapas bar that is popular and small. The food is delicious - favorites are authentic Bacaloa salt cod cakes, stuffed dates, jamon serrano, skirt steak, mussels and chorizo. Bring wine or buy it next door. The corkage fee is very low.
