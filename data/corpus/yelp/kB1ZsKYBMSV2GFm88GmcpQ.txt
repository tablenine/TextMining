Restaurant: Cafe Selmarie

This pleasant eatery in Lincoln Square next to the cool fountain is a good place for a light meal, but a very good place for a sweet treat. For food, have a quiche, salad, or one of the daily specials. Desserts include lemon bars, cookies, giant rum balls, and numerous cakes.
