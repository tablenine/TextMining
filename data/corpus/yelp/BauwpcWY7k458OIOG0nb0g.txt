They don't have the best sushi I've ever had, but it's certainly acceptable and the prices are reasonable.  And the Thai iced tea?  Well, I can't complain.  And the place itself is quite adorable once you get past the strip-mall exterior.  It's worth checking out if you're in the neighborhood.
