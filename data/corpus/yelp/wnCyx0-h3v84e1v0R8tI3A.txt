Restaurant: Ugo: An Italian Cafe
Category: Italian
The food is as good as I remember it when the first location opened up in West Los Angeles. The service is great and the ambiance is nice too. The prices are reasonable and it's a good place to take a date or to have a quiet, kick back dinner with friends. Their Puttanesca sauce is delicious.
