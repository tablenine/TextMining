Kopi was great.....but stay away from the veggie burger.  The bun was large and quite dry....it really ruined the burger.  The house salad was fantastic, the dijon vinaigrette is lovely.  The menu is large and there are a lot of vegetarian or vegan options.  Kopi is delightful!
