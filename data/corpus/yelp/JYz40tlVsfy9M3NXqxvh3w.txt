The 'Tis the Season (pumpkin) pancakes are amazing any time of year.  I also enjoyed the Barbeque Bob's Salad (served with warm cornbread and mouth-watering orange butter).   You will not regret The Griddle!  The only bummer is coming on the weekend and waiting for a table.
