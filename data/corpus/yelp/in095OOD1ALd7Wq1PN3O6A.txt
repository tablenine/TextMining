CUT was a great experience.  We had some of the most delicious and tenderest beef I've ever had.  However, the richness of the Wagyu gave me indigestion -  not the fault of the beef.  For that reason, I'm deducting one star, but if you have the stomach for it, it's probably a five star experience.
