OMG these burgers are off the proverbial chain! They are HUGE, so be forewarned - this is not for the faint of heart. They are definitely gigantic and delicious, and the hand cut fries are gluttony at its best. Not much in the way of ambience, but that's hardly the point here. Just big, messy tasty burgers and fries. Yum!
