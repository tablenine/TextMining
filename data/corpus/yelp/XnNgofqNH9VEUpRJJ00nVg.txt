Went back again for dinner and the margarita's were just as strong! I got the Pescado Veracruz. It's tilapia with mussels. It comes in a spicy, light sauce and was really good. It's a nice, healthy choice. The only thing was it seemed a little on the salty side. But I would would get it again.
