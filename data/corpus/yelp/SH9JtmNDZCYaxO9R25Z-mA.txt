A bit overrated.  Cocktails, pomegranate, tequila/jalapeño, were very good (@$16, they should) but martini with a twist is unimpressive.  My halibut was mostly salty, without much other nuance; the lamb was tough; the beef goulash was again predominately salty.  Also pricy.   Won't go back.
