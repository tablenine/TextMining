Restaurant: The Little Door

This is the most romantic restaurant in LA.  Arrive early and grab a drink at the bar then request a table either under the stars or near the fireplace.  A simple recommendation if you like fish; the Alaskan Halibut with a bouillabaise broth and aioli sauce.  Sooo tasty!!
