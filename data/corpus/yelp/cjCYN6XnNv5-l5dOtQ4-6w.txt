This place is my favorite restaurant in Los Angeles.  My fiance and I go here for every special occasion.  If they have Qupe Syrah, do try it.  Yum!  . . The food is seasonal but they almost always have goat cheese tarts of some sort.   HEAVEN.   The ribeye is to die for.
