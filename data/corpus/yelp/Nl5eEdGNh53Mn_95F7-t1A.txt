I still haven't found the best Mexican, but I'm a fan of Bone Garden.  It's not too expensive and loads better than Nuevo Laredo's runny beans.  The tacos may not have been spectacular, but they were damn good.  Authentic, this is not.  Tasty Tex-Mex for under $10?  Yes.
