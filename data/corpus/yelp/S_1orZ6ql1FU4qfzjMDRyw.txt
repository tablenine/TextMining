Restaurant: Los Nopales

The sauces here are incredible, and in many cases surpass the sauces at Topolobampo while costing a lot less money.  Chicken in mole and tilapia tacos were the real stars.  Shrimp in chipotle sauce a bit too cheesy and creamy for our taste, but still excellent.  The Ceviche was also tasty, though the really sweet kind (I prefer the acidity of lemons and limes).  Great chunky guac. Amazingly sweet and satisfying horchata. . I would definitely go back for more.
